Frequently Asked Question

What is datachable smart clothing?
Detachable smart clothing integrates smart sensors into garments, allowing users to monitor health, track location, or access other smart features. The "detachable" aspect means the tech components can be removed for washing or upgrades.
Who can get benefit from smart clothing?
Womens, fitness enthusiasts, elderly individuals, people with medical conditions, and parents (for child tracking).
Is my Data secure?
Yes, TechOwear uses encrypted and secure cloud storage to protect user data. You can manage privacy settings in the app.
What if my Device stops working?
TechOwear offers a warranty on its device. Contact customer support for troubleshooting or replacement.
What devices does it work with?
Most smart clothing syncs with Android devices via a mobile app. Check TechOwear’s specifications for compatibility.
