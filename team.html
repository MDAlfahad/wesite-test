About Us

What we do?

TechOwear is designed to address the critical issue of women’s safety by providing a smart, wearable solution that combines convenience and effectiveness. This project empowers women with a discreet, easily accessible safety tool that alerts authorities in emergencies and monitors health indicators, aiming to enhance personal security and peace of mind.

Spark of Innovation

The idea behind TechOwear was ignited by observing various natural calamities and emergencies that highlighted the need for rapid response and personal safety. Incidents like cave collapses, devastating floods in Indian states, and women’s safety concerns served as a wake-up call for the team. These events stirred a desire to create solutions that could save lives and empower individuals with advanced technology embedded in everyday wear.
